🫖 UK Account Types — Quick Reference

Cash ISA: Tax-free savings, up to £20,000/year combined ISA limit.
Stocks & Shares ISA: Tax-free investing, same £20,000 limit.
Lifetime ISA (LISA): Up to £4,000/year, government adds 25% bonus — usable for first home or age 60+. Counts toward £20k. Worth exploring once debt-free.
Easy Access Saver: Withdraw anytime, lower interest. Best for emergency buffer.
Junior ISA: Up to £9,000/year for a child, tax-free, locked until 18.
Premium Bonds: NS&I — no interest, monthly prize draws, capital protected.
Workplace Pension: Free money via employer match — separate from ISA limit.

Foundation first.

These are the documents and protections that make everything else safe. Tick them off as you sort each one. None of these need to wait for the debt to be cleared — they protect Tahlia now.

🩷 Will & Estate
Write a basic will
Online via Farewill (~£100), Will Aid (free in November in exchange for charity donation), or a local solicitor (£150–£250). Specify who inherits everything if you die.
Name a guardian for Tahlia in the will
The most important sentence in your will. Without this, a court decides who raises Tahlia. Have the conversation with the person first — make sure they accept.
Name a financial trustee for Tahlia's inheritance
Different from a guardian. The trustee manages money for Tahlia until she's 18 (or older, if you specify). Can be the same person as guardian, or different.
Store the will somewhere safe + tell someone where
A will no one can find isn't a will. Keep the original somewhere safe (solicitor, fireproof box, with the executor). Tell at least one trusted person where it is.
🌸 Insurance Cover
Get 3 quotes for term life insurance
Try Cavendish Online, LifeSearch, or Beagle Street. Aim for £200k–£300k cover, 20-year term. Should cost £8–£20/month for a healthy 29-year-old non-smoker.
Take out life insurance policy
Once you've compared, just pick one and start. You can always switch later. The cost of waiting another year is bigger than the cost of picking imperfectly.
Write the policy "in trust" for Tahlia
Free to do when you take out the policy. Means the payout goes directly to Tahlia (via your trustee), not into your estate where it can get caught up in probate or claimed against by creditors.
Get income protection insurance quotes
Different from life insurance — covers your income if you can't work due to illness/injury. LV=, Aviva, Royal London. Pick a deferment period (how long before it pays out) — longer = cheaper.
Take out income protection policy
Aim for cover that pays ~£1,500/mo if you're out of work. Would replace your income for 6–12 months while you sort yourself out.
💎 Power of Attorney
Lasting Power of Attorney — Health & Welfare
Names someone to make medical decisions if you can't. £82 to register with OPG. Critical for anyone with a child — without it, decisions about you (and indirectly Tahlia) get made by hospital staff.
Lasting Power of Attorney — Property & Finance
Names someone to manage your money if you can't. £82 to register. Means your bills get paid, Tahlia's school fees get sorted, even if you're hospitalised.

Tahlia's birth certificate (in a known place)
You'll need it for school, passports, benefits. Order a replacement from gov.uk if you don't have it (£11).
